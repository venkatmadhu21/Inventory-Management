Recieving and Storage

The storekeeper receives incoming shipments, checks their accuracy, and ensures they are properly stored in designated locations within the inventory storage area.

Record Keeping

They meticulously track inventory activities, recording receipts, withdrawals, and adjustments. This entails updating inventory databases promptly to reflect real-time stock levels and movements.

Inventory Control

Storekeepers regularly count inventory, identifying shortages and discrepancies, and reconcile them. They also monitor turnover rates and help set optimal stock levels to avoid shortages or excess stock.
