What is Demand form?

The demand form is used by HODs to request items. If available, items are issued; otherwise, an indent is generated for procurement.

How to Demand?

Select the "Demand Form" from the navigation bar, fill in the required table details including the quantity, and then click on the "Submit" button..

Submit Demand

After submission, the demand details are sent to the inventory clerk. If items are in stock, they're processed,otherwise, an indent is generated.
