What is Indent?

An indent form requests inventory procurement or replenishment, detailing item info, quantities, requester details, date, and instructions.

What is IV?

An issue voucher, or IV, is a brief document detailing an issued invoice, crucial for accounting and financial tracking.

What is RV?

A receipt voucher, or RV, is a concise document confirming received payment or goods, essential for accurate financial records.
